Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A timeless Italian classic, this creamy and savory pasta dish is ready in under 30 minutes, perfect for a quick and satisfying weeknight dinner.

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

15 mins

Servings

4

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Cook Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package directions until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare Sauce Base: While the pasta is cooking, whisk the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano in a medium bowl until well combined. Season generously with black pepper.
  3. Cook Pancetta: In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the diced pancetta until it's crispy and the fat has rendered. Remove from heat.
  4. Combine: Add the drained hot pasta to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss to coat the pasta in the rendered fat.
  5. Emulsify Sauce: Working quickly, pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, stirring vigorously. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce and prevent the eggs from scrambling. Continue to toss until the sauce thickens and coats the pasta.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately, garnished with extra Pecorino Romano and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
